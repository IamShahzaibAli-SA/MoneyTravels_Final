Procedure to Send Money Overseas through Money Travels

Registration

Go To The Money Travels website.

Complete the online registration form with:

  • Full Name
  • Date & Place of Birth
  • Complete Residencial Address (No PO BOX)
  • Contact Numbers (Phone, Mobile, Email)
  • Profession/Occupation
  • Valid Photo ID number + Expiry date

Upload:

  • Color scan copies of 100 Points Identification documents
  • Selfie with your Primary Photo ID (Driver License or Passport)

After submission, you'll receive an Account Activation Email with further instructions.

Account Activation

Money Travels will verify your ID and documents.

Once approved, you'll receive an Account Activation Confirmation Email.

Only Registered & Verified customers can send money.

Login & Create Remitance

Login at the Money Travels Customer Portal.
